What is Process Intelligence?

Process Intelligence is the discipline of understanding, measuring, and optimising how work actually flows through your organisation — in real time. It goes far beyond a process map drawn in a workshop; it connects live operational data to the processes that generate it.

Where traditional Business Intelligence answers "what happened?", Process Intelligence answers "why did it happen, which process caused it, and how do we fix it?" It closes the gap between your dashboards and your operations.

The result: organisations that can see bottlenecks before they become crises, predict outcomes from process behaviour, and continuously improve without waiting for quarterly reviews.

Real-Time Visibility

See exactly where work is stuck — right now, not at the end of month.

Root Cause Analysis

Link outcomes directly to the process steps that caused them.

Predictive Signals

Identify patterns in process data before they turn into problems.

Continuous Improvement

Measure the impact of every change — not just once, but continuously.

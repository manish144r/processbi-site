OUR PROCESS

How It Works

From messy data to clear decisions in 4 steps

Step 1

Discovery Call

A free 45-minute conversation. No sales pitch — just a frank discussion about where your data and processes are today, what outcomes you need, and whether we're the right fit. We come prepared with questions, not decks.

What happens in this step

  • Understand your current data landscape and pain points
  • Define what "success" looks like for your organisation
  • Agree whether and how to proceed — no pressure
Duration: 45 minutes  ·  Cost: Free

What happens in this step

  • Inventory all data sources, systems, and integration points
  • Map current-state processes with BPMN swim-lane diagrams
  • Design the target data architecture (star schema, Fabric/SQL)
  • Deliver written scope, timeline, and fixed-fee proposal
Duration: 1–2 weeks  ·  Output: Architecture blueprint
Step 2

Data Audit & Architecture

We get into your systems and understand exactly what data exists, what quality it's in, and what architecture is needed to make it reliable. No assumptions — only documented evidence. The output is a signed-off blueprint before any build begins.

Step 3

Dashboard Build

We build the data pipelines, warehouse, and Power BI reports against the signed-off architecture. Iterative delivery in sprints — you see working dashboards every 2 weeks, not a 3-month black box. UAT at every milestone.

What happens in this step

  • Build ETL/ELT pipelines from source systems to data warehouse
  • Create semantic model and DAX measures in Power BI
  • 2-week sprint delivery with UAT sign-off at each milestone
  • Row-level security, sharing, and workspace governance configured
Duration: 4–12 weeks  ·  Output: Live dashboards

What happens in this step

  • Live training sessions for report users and data owners
  • Full documentation: data dictionary, model guide, pipeline notes
  • 30-day hypercare period after go-live
  • Optional ongoing retainer for continuous improvement
Duration: 1–2 weeks  ·  Output: Self-sufficient team
Step 4

Training & Handover

We don't disappear at go-live. Your team gets live training, full documentation, and a 30-day hypercare period so they're genuinely self-sufficient. The goal is to build your internal capability — not dependency on us.

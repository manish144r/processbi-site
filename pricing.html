THE PROCESS

How We Engage

Every client relationship follows a clear, low-friction path — from first conversation to ongoing delivery.

STEP 1

Discovery Call

A focused 45-minute conversation — no sales pitch. We listen to your challenge, ask the right questions, and determine whether we're the right fit for your organisation.

STEP 2

Scoping & Proposal

We map out what's needed — objectives, data sources, timelines, and deliverables. You receive a clear written proposal before any commitment is made.

STEP 3

Statement of Work

Once the scope is agreed, we formalise it in a Statement of Work. Everything is documented — deliverables, timeline, responsibilities, and review points. No surprises.

STEP 4

Delivery

We get to work. Regular check-ins keep you informed at every stage. Senior practitioners only — your project is never handed to a junior resource.

STEP 5

Ongoing Support

After delivery, many clients choose to continue the relationship — for iterative improvements, retainer-based support, or new workstreams. That conversation happens naturally.

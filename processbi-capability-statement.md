## Services

### 1. Process Discovery Workshop
**Investment: $5,000 – $15,000 | Duration: 1–2 days**

A structured facilitated workshop to map your current-state processes from the people who live them.

- Pre-workshop process inventory and stakeholder interviews
- Facilitated workshop with process owners and operators
- Structured capture using BPMN 2.0 notation
- As-is process map delivered within 5 business days
- Identification of pain points, handoff failures, and bottlenecks
- Output: swimlane process diagram, preliminary RACI, issues log

*Ideal for: organisations beginning a process review, teams preparing for audit or compliance, leaders who need to understand what is actually happening vs. what procedures say*

---

### 2. Process Map + Automation Blueprint
**Investment: $15,000 – $40,000 | Duration: 3–4 weeks**

Our core engagement — the full process intelligence cycle from discovery to automation roadmap.

**Phase 1 — Discovery (Week 1):** Stakeholder workshops, as-is capture, process inventory
**Phase 2 — Mapping (Week 2):** BPMN swimlane diagrams, RACI matrix, SOP generation
**Phase 3 — Assessment (Week 2–3):** Each process step scored: automatable / human-in-loop / human-only
**Phase 4 — Blueprint (Week 3–4):** Tool-specific automation recommendations sequenced by ROI

Deliverables include:
- Visio-compatible BPMN process maps
- Automation opportunity register (prioritised by ROI and implementation ease)
- Tool-specific implementation blueprint (Power Automate, ServiceNow, Python/API, RPA)
- APRA CPS 230 compliance mapping (material business services identification, tolerance metrics)
- Executive briefing deck

*Ideal for: APRA-regulated entities (banks, super funds, insurers) requiring CPS 230 documentation; enterprises post-Nintex EOL migration; organisations beginning a digital transformation programme*

Gap Analysis & Root Cause

DMAIC root-cause analysis that pinpoints where processes lose time, money or quality, and quantifies the cost of waste.

What this service is

Symptoms get treated. Causes don't. DMAIC root-cause analysis goes past the firefighting to find what's actually driving cost, delay or quality loss — and how much it costs you.

How we deliver it

Each engagement follows a clear, repeatable shape.

01
Define the gap in measurable terms — not adjectives.
02
Measure the process as it really runs, not as it's described.
03
Analyse with the right statistical tool — fishbone, Pareto, 5-Whys, control charts.
04
Quantify the cost of the gap so the case for change writes itself.

What you get

The tangible outputs you'll have at the end.

  • ✓ A defensible root-cause analysis aligned to DMAIC
  • ✓ A quantified cost-of-waste figure backed by data
  • ✓ Prioritised improvement opportunities
  • ✓ A control plan to prevent the problem returning

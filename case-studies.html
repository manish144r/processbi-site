Government & Public Sector

Process Intelligence: Finding Operational Inefficiency in the Data

A government agency knew their citizen-services throughput was above the policy target but could not identify where. Their process documentation described how the process was designed to work — not how it actually worked. We extracted event logs, ran process mining to reconstruct the real process, identified eleven undocumented variants, and located the primary bottleneck at a specific handover point invisible in headline statistics.

Approach: Process discovery from data — conformance checking, variant analysis, rework loop identification — then a redesigned BPMN for the primary path.
Outcome: Real process map reconstructed. Bottleneck quantified. Two rework loops root-caused and addressed without system change.
Case Management Logs→Process Mining→Variant Analysis→Power BI Dashboard

## Challenge
Financially literate individuals and small business owners are building personal finance dashboards manually in Excel — a time-intensive process requiring custom formulas, chart configurations, and theme design. These dashboards:

- Are static (no live data feeds)
- Require rebuilding when source data changes
- Lack collaborative or sharing capabilities
- Cannot scale to multi-account or multi-entity views
- Are shared informally via email on request, with no secure delivery mechanism

The LinkedIn post demonstrates clear demand: 116 reactions and 37 comment-requests for the dashboard file within hours of posting, indicating a significant unmet need for a shareable, reusable personal finance solution.

---

## Solution
**RayFin**, powered by the **ProcessBI methodology**, delivers a production-grade Personal Finance Intelligence platform on Microsoft Fabric + Power BI that replaces manual Excel builds with:

- **Live data pipelines** pulling from bank feeds, CSV uploads, or Open Banking APIs via Fabric Dataflows
- **Semantic model** with pre-built DAX measures for Balance, Inflow/Outflow, Goal Tracking, Budget vs Actuals, and Cost Structure
- **Multi-theme Power BI report** (Dark + Light) replicating and extending the Excel dashboard aesthetic
- **ProcessBI 3D Deploy methodology** ensuring the solution is Deployable, Documented, and Delivered within a defined sprint
- **Secure sharing** via Power BI workspace / embedded app — no manual email distribution of files

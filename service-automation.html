What we do

Manual data entry, copy-paste between systems, email-driven approvals, spreadsheet reconciliation — these are the invisible costs that accumulate across every team. ProcessBI identifies and automates the workflows that cost your business the most time, using Microsoft Power Automate, Azure Logic Apps, and Fabric Data Factory to build reliable, auditable automation.

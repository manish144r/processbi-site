## 1. Project Description

Acme Fabrications Pty Ltd is a NSW-based manufacturer with 45 employees and annual revenue of approximately $8,000,000. The business currently relies on manual, spreadsheet-based processes to consolidate data from its ERP, production management system, and other operational sources. This creates significant reporting delays, data quality issues, and limits management's ability to make timely, data-driven decisions.

This project involves the implementation of **Microsoft Fabric** as a unified cloud data platform, with **Microsoft Power BI** as the visualisation and reporting layer. Microsoft Fabric data warehouse to consolidate production data from ERP (MYOB Acumatica) and MES systems, replacing manual spreadsheet reporting. Power BI dashboards for OEE, yield, inventory turns, and on-time delivery. Includes automated ETL pipelines with daily refresh.

The platform will consolidate all production, inventory, financial, and sales data into a single source of truth, enabling real-time dashboards and automated reporting to replace current manual processes.

## 3. Manufacturing Modernisation and Technology Adoption

This project directly modernises Acme Fabrications Pty Ltd's manufacturing operations by introducing a unified digital intelligence layer. The Fabric data platform replaces manual, spreadsheet-based reporting with automated, real-time manufacturing KPI monitoring.

**Technologies being adopted:**
- Microsoft Fabric (cloud-native data lakehouse)
- Power BI Premium (enterprise analytics and dashboards)
- Azure Data Factory / Fabric Data Factory (automated ETL pipelines)
- Delta Lake (ACID-compliant data storage)
- Integration with existing ERP and MES systems

**Manufacturing KPIs enabled:**
- Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) in real-time
- Production yield and first-pass quality rates
- Material consumption vs. standard cost variance
- Inventory turns and raw material aging
- On-time delivery vs. production schedule

## 4. Expected Outcomes and Benefits

The project will deliver the following measurable outcomes for Acme Fabrications Pty Ltd:

| Outcome | Projected Impact |
|---|---|
| Manual reporting hours | Reduction in manual reporting effort by 70%, saving 80 FTE-hours per month |
| Data visibility | Real-time visibility into production KPIs (OEE, yield, waste) enabling faster decisions |
| Financial saving | Estimated $80,000 p.a. in avoided manual data handling costs |
| Data quality | Reduction in data errors through automated ETL pipelines replacing spreadsheet reconciliation |

**Employment Impact:**
The project is not expected to result in significant direct headcount additions. However, by freeing approximately 2 FTE currently engaged in manual reporting, the business intends to redeploy those staff to higher-value analytical and customer-facing roles, improving overall productivity and business capability.

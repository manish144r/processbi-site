Platform Migration

Synapse → Databricks Migration

A staged migration onto a governed Databricks Lakehouse — operational reporting never went dark.

Azure SynapseDatabricksDelta LakePower BI

The challenge

Synapse workloads had grown past where they made sense, and Databricks was the right next platform — but the operations team couldn't tolerate any downtime in reporting.

Our approach

How we structured the work, end to end.

01
Audited Synapse workloads to plan a wave-based migration with no break in reporting continuity.
02
Re-architected the Lakehouse on Databricks with Delta Lake and governance baked in.
03
Used migration tooling to replicate logic faithfully and reconcile against parallel-running Synapse outputs.
04
Cut over reporting wave-by-wave with full parallel running until the new platform proved itself.

What we do

Most organisations invest in technology before understanding the processes it needs to serve. The result? Expensive tools that replicate broken workflows. ProcessBI takes a different approach — we start with your people, your decisions, and your information flows. Only then do we recommend systems that genuinely improve the way work gets done.

Our Approach

01
Discover
Workshops, interviews and process observation to understand current-state reality.
02
Analyse
Root-cause analysis, value-stream mapping and quantifying the cost of inefficiency.
03
Design
Co-create future-state processes with the people who will use them.
04
Implement
Support change management, tooling decisions, and ongoing measurement.

End-to-End Process Mapping

We document every step, decision, handoff, and exception — giving you a clear picture of how work actually flows today.

Lean & Six Sigma Analysis

We identify waste, bottlenecks, and duplication using structured methodology — not guesswork.

Future-State Design

Technology-Ready Specifications

We produce process documentation that directly informs your data architecture, automation rules, and BI requirements.

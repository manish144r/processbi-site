The ProcessBI Story

ProcessBI was founded by Manish Sharma after 15+ years delivering enterprise data, analytics, and AI projects for major Australian organisations across financial services, government, energy, and manufacturing.

The name says it: Process + BI. Sustainable data platforms require both — business intelligence without sound data processes fails, and data processes without actionable intelligence wastes everyone's time. Every engagement connects both sides.

In 2025, ProcessBI became an AI-native operation with the launch of the AI Research Engine — an internal multi-agent AI infrastructure where 300+ autonomous agents run continuously: researching markets, compounding knowledge, generating proposals, and monitoring the competitive landscape. The same patterns we deploy for clients, running our own business.

ProcessBI operates lean: one senior architect, trusted specialist partners, no overhead, no bench costs. Clients get direct access to the person doing the work — not a relationship manager passing notes to a junior team.

How We Work

Principles That Drive Every Engagement

Outcomes, Not Outputs

A Power BI dashboard is not a deliverable — reduced reporting cycle time, freed analyst hours, and caught fraud are. Every engagement is scoped to a business outcome with a measurable metric attached. If we can't define success before we start, we don't start.

Honest Architecture

No vendor allegiances. Fabric is not always the answer. Snowflake is sometimes right. Sometimes you don't need ML at all. The first thing I do in any engagement is challenge the existing assumptions — not validate them. Honest architecture assessments, even when they're uncomfortable.

Senior Delivery Only

Every line of code, every design decision, every client meeting — handled at principal level. No juniors learning on your budget, no handoffs mid-project. When you brief me, I'm the one building it. Small team, zero dilution.

AI-Native Operations

The AI Research Engine isn't a marketing story — it's how ProcessBI runs. 300+ AI agents do the research, analysis, and content work that used to take days. This gives clients access to higher-quality strategic thinking, faster, at lower cost. We eat our own cooking.

Knowledge Transfer, Not Dependency

The goal is a client team that can maintain what we build, not perpetual reliance on ProcessBI. Every engagement includes architecture documentation, runbooks, and — where useful — developer training. Your capability grows; the relationship evolves to higher-value work.

Australian Enterprise Context

APRA CPS 234, RG271, Privacy Act, and the specific regulatory environment of Australian financial services, government, and utilities. No need to translate global frameworks — we know the Australian compliance landscape and build it in from day one.

The autonomous intelligence layer powering ProcessBI around the clock. Continuously researching, synthesising, and compounding knowledge — so every client engagement starts with deeper insight than the last.

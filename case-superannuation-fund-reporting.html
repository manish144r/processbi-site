Superannuation Fund Reporting

Member, contribution and investment reporting on one reconciled model — regulatory packs and member statements produced from a single, defensible source.

Power BIData ModellingReconciliationAPRA Reporting

The challenge

Fund reporting was assembled by hand each period — administration, custodian and investment data stitched together in spreadsheets. Member statements, board packs and regulatory submissions each drew on slightly different cuts, so reconciling them consumed days and left room for error in numbers that have to be exactly right.

Our approach

How we structured the work.

01
Brought administration, custodian and investment feeds into one reconciled model with daily control totals, so every downstream report ties back to the same figures.
02
Built the reporting layer around the fund's real structures — options, member segments, unit prices — rather than a generic template.
03
Automated the recurring packs: member statement inputs, board reporting and regulatory tables generated from the single source instead of re-keyed.
04
Kept a clear reconciliation and lineage trail so any number in a regulatory pack can be traced back to source.
